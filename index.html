The Field

The world he comes from.

The Field is the source of all imagination. A liminal white space where ideas first exist as fragments of light and shape. It answers to attention: focus on it and the fragments stabilize into form, look away and they dissolve. It belongs to everyone.

The Name

LUCA means two things.

A bringer of light, and the root that everything branches from.

Meaning one

Bringer of light

The physical light of projection and LEDs, and the inner light of awareness and creation. Light, externally through art and internally through self-realization.

Meaning two

Last Universal Common Ancestor

In science, LUCA is the single root that every living thing descends from. The Field works the same way: one shared source, and everything imaginable branches out of it.

One source. Endless branches.
