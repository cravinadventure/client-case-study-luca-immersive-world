LUCA IMMERSIVE · World Design

The Field.

The world LUCA comes from. A shared, infinite source of imagination.

The Field is the source of all imagination. A liminal white space, neither digital nor physical, where ideas first exist as fragments of light and shape. It answers to attention: focus on it and the fragments stabilize into form, look away and they dissolve. It belongs to everyone, and what you pull from it depends only on how clearly you can translate what you find.

Who Is In The Field

Four roles, one loop.

The source

The Field

The undefined white void where all ideas begin. Common property, accessible to anyone who can imagine.

The guide

LUCA

The Field's light given a form. A translator who reveals what is already there and hands it to people. A host, not a creator.

The maker

The Receiver

The human conduit who catches what LUCA reveals and builds it into the real world: visuals, stages, stories. Defined by receptivity, not genius.

The protagonist

The Viewer

You. Never passive, your attention already shapes The Field. The goal is to realize "this is mine too" and become the next maker.

Inside

A white world, shaped by attention.

The Point

Creativity is a shared resource.

The whole world is built on one idea: imagination is infinite and common, reached through a guide, and meant to be handed on. The viewer becomes a maker too.
